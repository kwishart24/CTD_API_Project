Seasons in Time:

Exploring History Through Weather and Art

Enter in a date between 01/01/1940 and 10/15/2025. Then enter a latitude (-90 to 90) and longitude (-180 to 180) to find the historical weather data of that location on that day. An art piece with information will also generate from the Art Insitute of Chicago that represents the type of weather that the chosen location was experiencing on that day.
